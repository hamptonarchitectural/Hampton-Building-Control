How To Engage Us

A process designed to help your practice.

1

Instruct

You instruct us to proceed and send us your plans and any other information in DWG, RVT or PDF.

2

Retainer

We take an initial payment equal to 40% of the final fee, which we hold on account and draw-down at our standard hourly rate.

3

Production

We produce the documentation you need in the format you require. This varies depending on your requirement.

4

Approval

We issue watermarked versions of your documents via a shared drive to varify during the process.

5

Payments

We invoice every 30 days based on progress or the full final amount due, whichever comes sooner.

6

Completion

We recieve the final payment and issue the final details via a shared drive, ready for you to take the next step.

We Talk Your Language

Our go-to software is the industry leading Building Information Modelling software Revit, and can therefore provide you with RVT files if you need it. If your practice uses AutoCAD, not a problem. Our details have all been developed in Revit as a starting point, it's easier to go back than forward so we export all of our details to a DWG format, and top-and-tail them so they work perfectly in any version of AutoCAD.


We can adopt your CAD standards or use any of your pre-existing templates to make the process as simple as possible.
